




# Create TradeFlow Feature Graphic - Step by Step

## Option 1: Canva (Easiest - 15 minutes)

### Step 1: Go to Canva
1. Go to: **canva.com**
2. Sign in (or create free account)

### Step 2: Create Custom Size
1. Click **"Create a design"** (top right)
2. Click **"Custom size"**
3. Enter width: **1024**
4. Enter height: **500**  
5. Select **px** (pixels)
6. Click **"Create new design"**

### Step 3: Set Blue Background
1. Click anywhere on the canvas
2. Look at left sidebar
3. Click **"Background color"** 
4. Click **"+"** next to color swatches
5. In the hex field, type: **0b3ea8**
6. Press Enter

✓ Your canvas is now blue!

### Step 4: Add Your TF Icon
1. Go to your desktop where you saved "TradeFlow Logo.png"
2. In Canva, click **"Uploads"** (left sidebar)
3. Click **"Upload files"**
4. Select your TradeFlow Logo.png
5. Click on the uploaded image to add it to canvas
6. Resize it smaller (drag corner handles)
7. Position it on the **LEFT side** of the banner
8. Make it about 200-250 pixels tall

### Step 5: Add "TradeFlow" Text
1. Click **"Text"** in left sidebar
2. Click **"Add a heading"**
3. Type: **TradeFlow**
4. Select the text
5. In the top toolbar:
   - **Font:** Choose a bold sans-serif like:
     - Montserrat Bold
     - Poppins Bold
     - Roboto Bold
   - **Size:** Make it large - around 80-100
   - **Color:** Click color box
     - Enter hex: **f97316** (orange)
6. **Position:** Place it to the RIGHT of your TF icon
7. **Center vertically** on the canvas

### Step 6: Add Subtitle
1. Click **"Text"** again
2. Click **"Add a subheading"**
3. Type: **Professional Workforce Management**
4. Select the text
5. Style it:
   - **Font:** Same as "TradeFlow" or a lighter weight
   - **Size:** Smaller - around 28-36
   - **Color:** White (#FFFFFF) or Light Gray
6. **Position:** Place it directly UNDER "TradeFlow" text
7. **Align:** Left-align with "TradeFlow"

### Step 7: Adjust & Perfect
1. **Select all elements** (Ctrl+A or Cmd+A)
2. Use top toolbar to:
   - **Align center vertically** (if needed)
   - Make sure everything looks balanced
3. The TF icon should be on the left
4. "TradeFlow" and subtitle should be to the right of icon
5. Leave some breathing room - don't cram to edges

### Step 8: Download
1. Click **"Share"** button (top right)
2. Click **"Download"**
3. Format: **PNG**
4. Click **"Download"**

✓ Your feature graphic is ready!

## Design Tips:

### ✅ Do:
- Keep text LARGE and readable
- Use high contrast (orange on blue)
- Leave white space - don't crowd
- Make sure "TradeFlow" dominates
- Keep it simple and clean

### ❌ Don't:
- Use more than 2-3 colors
- Make text too small
- Crowd everything together
- Use thin fonts
- Add too many elements
