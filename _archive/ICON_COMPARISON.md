```
│       (smaller text)            │

```

### ⚠️ Cons:
- **Text becomes unreadable** at small sizes (48x48)
- **TF has to be smaller** to fit text underneath
- **Cluttered appearance** - too much going on
- **Less impactful** - dilutes the bold TF
- **Text blurs** when icon is small

### How it appears on phone:
- **192x192 (Home screen)** - Text readable, TF smaller
- **96x96 (App drawer)** - Text barely readable, TF harder to see
- **48x48 (Notification)** - Text completely illegible, looks messy

## Real-World Examples

### Icons WITHOUT Text (Successful)
- **Instagram** - Just the camera gradient square
- **Twitter** - Just the bird
- **Facebook** - Just the "f"
- **YouTube** - Just the play button
- **Spotify** - Just the circle with sound waves
- **Apple Music** - Just the note

### Icons WITH Text (Less Common)
- Usually look cluttered
- Text often illegible at small sizes
- Reserved for logos, not app icons
