## Part 2: How to Host It (FREE)

### Option A: GitHub Pages (RECOMMENDED - 5 minutes)

**Super easy and professional!**

#### Step 1: Create GitHub Account
1. Go to **github.com**
2. Click "Sign up"
3. Create free account (if you don't have one)

#### Step 2: Create Repository
1. Click **"+"** (top right)
2. Click **"New repository"**
3. Repository name: **tradeflow-privacy**
4. Make it **Public**
5. Check ✓ **"Add a README file"**
6. Click **"Create repository"**

#### Step 3: Add Privacy Policy
1. Click **"Add file"** → **"Create new file"**
2. File name: **index.html**
3. **Copy the entire HTML above** and paste it
4. Scroll down, click **"Commit changes"**

#### Step 4: Enable GitHub Pages
1. Click **"Settings"** (top menu)
2. Click **"Pages"** (left sidebar)
3. Under "Source", select **"main"** branch
4. Click **"Save"**
5. Wait 1-2 minutes
6. Your URL will be: **https://yourusername.github.io/tradeflow-privacy/**

✅ **Done! Copy that URL for Play Store!**

---

### Option B: Google Sites (ALSO EASY - 5 minutes)

#### Step 1: Go to Google Sites
1. Go to **sites.google.com**
2. Sign in with your Google account

#### Step 2: Create New Site
1. Click **"Blank"** template
2. Site name: **TradeFlow Privacy Policy**

#### Step 3: Add Content
1. Click in the main area
2. Click **"Text box"**
3. **Copy the text part** of the privacy policy above (not the HTML tags)
4. Paste it
5. Format headings as needed

#### Step 4: Publish
1. Click **"Publish"** (top right)
2. Choose URL: **tradeflow-privacy**
3. Click **"Publish"**
4. Your URL: **https://sites.google.com/view/tradeflow-privacy**

✅ **Done!**
