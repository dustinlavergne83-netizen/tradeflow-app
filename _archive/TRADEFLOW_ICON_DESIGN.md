## Quick Design with Canva (Recommended - 5 Minutes)

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Go to Canva.com** and sign in

2. **Create Custom Size:**
   - Click "Create a design"
   - Choose "Custom size"
   - Enter: 1024 x 1024 pixels
   - Click "Create new design"

3. **Set Blue Background:**
   - Click anywhere on the canvas
   - In left sidebar, click "Background color"
   - Click "+" next to color picker
   - Enter hex code: `#0b3ea8`
   - Press Enter

4. **Add "TF" Text:**
   - Click "Text" in left sidebar
   - Click "Add a heading"
   - Type: `TF`
   - Select the text

5. **Format the Text:**
   - **Font:** Choose a bold sans-serif font like:
     - "Montserrat Bold"
     - "Poppins Bold"
     - "Bebas Neue"
     - "Oswald Bold"
   - **Size:** Make it LARGE - around 300-400
   - **Color:** 
     - Select text
     - Click color picker
     - Enter: `#f97316`
   - **Italic:**
     - With text selected
     - Click the "I" button (italics)
     - Or use font style dropdown

6. **Position:**
   - Center the "TF" perfectly
   - Use the center alignment guides
   - Make sure it's visually balanced

7. **Optional Enhancements:**
   - Add subtle shadow for depth
   - Slight gradient on text
   - Small electrical/flow element

8. **Download:**
   - Click "Share" button (top right)
   - Click "Download"
   - Format: PNG
   - Download

## Alternative: Figma (More Professional)

### Steps:

1. Go to **figma.com** and create free account

2. **Create New Design:**
   - Click "New design file"
   - Press "F" (frame tool)
   - Enter dimensions: 1024 x 1024

3. **Blue Background:**
   - Select frame
   - In right panel, click "Fill"
   - Enter: `#0b3ea8`

4. **Add Text:**
   - Press "T" for text tool
   - Click in center
   - Type: `TF`

5. **Style Text:**
   - Select text
   - **Font:** Montserrat or Poppins
   - **Weight:** Bold
   - **Size:** 300-400
   - **Color:** `#f97316`
   - **Italic:** Click "I" button in text properties

6. **Center Perfectly:**
   - Select text
   - In top toolbar:
     - Click "Align horizontal centers"
     - Click "Align vertical centers"

7. **Export:**
   - Select frame
   - In right panel, click "Export"
   - Format: PNG
   - 1x (1024x1024)
   - Click "Export"

## Design Tips

### Do's:
✅ Make "TF" LARGE - should fill 60-70% of icon
✅ Use perfect centering
✅ Keep it simple - just blue + orange "TF"
✅ Ensure high contrast for visibility

### Don'ts:

❌ Don't use gradients (keep it flat)
❌ Don't make text too small
❌ Don't use thin fonts
❌ Don't overcomplicate
