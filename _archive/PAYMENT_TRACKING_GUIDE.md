# Payment Tracking System - Phase 1 🎯

## What We're Building

Transform your invoices from simple billing to complete cash flow management!

---

## Step 1: Run the SQL ✅

1. Go to **Supabase Dashboard** → SQL Editor
2. Open the file: **`ADD_PAYMENT_TRACKING.sql`**
3. Copy and paste into SQL Editor
4. Click **Run**

This adds these fields to your invoices:
- `payment_status` - unpaid, partial, or paid
- `payment_date` - when payment was received
- `payment_method` - check, cash, credit card, ACH, wire, other
- `amount_paid` - how much has been paid
- `payment_notes` - any notes about the payment
